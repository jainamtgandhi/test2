GTM Strategy

Comprehensive go-to-market strategies including product launch campaigns, channel strategy, and distribution planning. Focused on driving adoption and revenue growth through strategic execution.

Key Deliverables

  • Product launch strategy and execution
  • Channel strategy and distribution
  • Pricing strategy and market entry
  • Launch campaign management

Strategy Framework

Launch Timeline

Developed comprehensive 90-day launch timeline with pre-launch, launch, and post-launch phases.

Channel Mix

  • Owned channels: Website, email, social media
  • Earned channels: PR, influencer partnerships
  • Paid channels: Digital advertising, sponsored content

  • Coordinated with sales and customer success for rollout timing
  • Established KPIs and measurement framework

Tools Used: HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Ads, Facebook Ads
